# BMC Helix — End-to-End Incident Management  
## Executive Summary (Petronas)

**Prepared for:** Petronas · **Scenario:** Production Operations Visibility & Reporting  
**Document:** Demo design · **Version:** 1.0 · **Date:** 2025-03-21

### Situation

Operations and engineering teams depend on **timely, accurate production visibility** (dashboards, deferment data, KPIs). When that **digital business service** degrades—stale figures, missing categories, intermittent timeouts—**uncertainty spreads**: users cannot tell if the issue is local, network-wide, or in the **data pipeline**. The **service desk**, **IT operations**, and **application/data teams** must align quickly while **leadership** needs a **clear, auditable** narrative.

### What this demo proves

A **single thread** from **first business symptom** to **restored service and learning**, using **BMC Helix Digital Workplace (DWP)** as the **front door**, **BMC Helix ITSM** as the **system of action**, **BMC Helix AIOps** for **sense-making** on events and metrics, and **AI Agents** as **assistants** at each stage—accelerating triage, communication, and knowledge without replacing governance.

---

| Platform | Role in the story |
|----------|-------------------|
| **Helix DWP** | Self-service entry, **service context**, catalog, **AI-guided** answers |
| **Helix ITSM** | Incident, **priority**, tasks, **CMDB** relationships, SLAs, closure |
| **Helix AIOps** | **Correlation**, anomaly context, **probable cause** signals |
| **AI Agents** | Natural-language **triage**, **draft comms**, **KB** suggestions, exec-friendly **summaries** |

---

### Outcomes Petronas can recognize

- **Faster time-to-understand** — One **service-centric** story instead of tool-hopping  
- **Fewer noisy escalations** — **AIOps** collapses alert volume into **actionable** insight  
- **Better stakeholder communication** — **AI-assisted** updates grounded in **ticket + service** context  
- **Sustainable improvement** — **Problem/knowledge** loop with **DWP** deflection next time
